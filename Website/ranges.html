Ranges & Facilities

Kelowna & District Fish and Game Club offers a variety of world-class ranges and facilities, designed for safety, accessibility, and enjoyment of all members and guests.

Outdoor Rifle Range

  • 100m, 200m, and 300m lanes
  • Covered shooting benches
  • Target stands provided
  • Ear/eye protection required

Pistol Range

  • 25m lanes for handguns
  • Steel and paper targets
  • Handgun Safety Qualification required
  • IPSC and PPC events hosted regularly

Archery Range

  • Outdoor and indoor lanes
  • 3D targets and competitions
  • BCAA-sanctioned events
  • Beginner to advanced archers welcome

Trap & Skeet Range

  • Fully automated trap machines
  • Instruction for beginners available
  • Weekly league nights
